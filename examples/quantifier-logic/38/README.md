# 38 - More translations for practice

This example is just to give you more practice translating sentences of various sorts.
They are all true in `SkolemWorld`, in case you want to look while translating.
Translate the following sentences.

1. Not every square is smaller than every triangle.
2. No square is to the right of anything.
3. There is a circle unless there are at least two big objects.
4. No square with nothing above it is smaller than another square.
5. If any circles are small, then they are between two squares.
6. If a square is medium or is above something medium, then it
  has nothing to its right except for triangles.
7. The further above a thing is, the bigger it is.
8. Everything is the same size as something else.
9. Every square has a triangle of the same size to its right.
10. Nothing is the same size as two (or more) other things.
11. Nothing is between objects of shapes other than its own.

- Open `SkolemWorld` by running `runQ38`.
- Notice that all of the above English sentences are true.
- Verify that the same holds of your translations.
- This time, make changes to `SkolemWorld` and see that the truth
  value of your translations track that of the English sentence.
- For example, consider sentence 5.
- Add a small circle between the bottom two squares.
- The English sentence 5 is still true. Is your translation?
- Now move the circle over between two triangles.
- The English sentence 5 is false. Is your translation?
- Now make the circle medium.
- The English sentence 5 is again true. How about your translation?
