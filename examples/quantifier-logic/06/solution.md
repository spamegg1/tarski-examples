# 06 - solution

- Translation of "There is a triangle that is big" is
  ∃x (Tri(x) ∧ Big(x)) (second sentence).
- Translation of "There is a square between a and b" is
  ∃x (Sqr(x) ∧ Btw(x,a,b)) (ninth sentence).
- Translation of "There is a big circle"? is
  ∃x (Cir(x) ∧ Big(x)) (the last sentence).
